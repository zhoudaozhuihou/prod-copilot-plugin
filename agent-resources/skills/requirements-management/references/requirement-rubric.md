# Requirement Quality Rubric

A requirement is high quality when it is:

- Clear: one interpretation for product, engineering, QA, and stakeholders.
- Valuable: tied to a business goal or user need.
- Bounded: in-scope and out-of-scope are explicit.
- Testable: acceptance criteria are objective.
- Traceable: links to story, API/UI/data/test/release artifacts.
- Governed: security, privacy, audit, data, and compliance impact is known.
- Sequenced: dependencies and MVP/phasing are explicit.
